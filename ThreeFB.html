Design and Fabrication of a Three-Flyball Centrifugal Governer

This project is primarily based on taking up a design process, analysing it, performing hand calculations and analysis, creating a model and then using CAE to validate the design. In this case, the product is a 3-flyball Watt governor.

While the more common version of the Watt governor is the 2-flyball version, this project analysis both the design by creating them from the ground up, by performing all requirement judgements and calculations and analysis, both using mental ability and Computer Aided Engineering to solve the problems using 3D meshes and boundary constraints.

This also includes performing a motion study of the produced design on the software using appropriate constraints. The final results acknowledge the fact that the 3-flyball version would definitely lead to a reduction in the maximum stress produced at various parts of the machine and thus, would be a better choice structurally, if not monetarily. The two flyball version designed entails two 5 Kg flyballs whereas the three flyball version entails both 3 flyballs with three 5 Kg flyballs and and three flyballs with three 3.3 Kg flyballs.

The result concludes that the three flyball with 5 Kg flyballs each would turn out to be the optimum choice in case for longevity and structural performance.

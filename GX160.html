The objective of the project is to tackle these very problems listed and develop a prototype of a new ergonomic chair. Our ergonomic chair aims to allow user to work or play for long hours without compromising their health. Our ergonomic chair prototypes an advanced lumbar support understanding human posture, body pains and anthropometry best suited to the average user in today’s time. Along with this, we prototype a stable laptop holder to provide a mobile workstation without compromising the comfort of a table-chair combination. The laptop holder along with the arm-rest is removable and adjustable to provide for individual preferences. It uses the quick pin mechanism. Along with this, the prototype also includes locking castor wheels, which prevent the slippery nature of wheels in present chair.

During the present time in Covid, a lot of people are working from home. In such a situation, our prototype chair aims to be the most feasible solution. It takes into consideration of all the comfort points of a user and serves to be an economic option as well in comparison to the other chairs available with the same features, estimated to have a pricing of Rs12,000. We tend to see a huge market for the chair, as we realized lot of people who are in a word-from-home situation don’t have the table-chair combination present at home.

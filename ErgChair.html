Chair With Laptop

I designed this chair as a part of my final year capstone project. The spread of Covid-19 saw a widespread increase in the culture of working from home. People now sit for long hours at a stretch on a chair-table combo and work. After conducting a survey of around 100+ people and multiple interviews, it was concluded that most people do not have a comfortable chair at home for them to sit in for many hours, and those who had so, felt that sitting at the same place for the majority of the day got monotonous. This chair prototype aimed to resolve both of these issues. After careful consideration and selection of dimensions using anthropometric measurements, this chair was designed to provide both comfortability and mobility. It has an ergonomic structure, removable and adjustable armrests and laptop pad and locking caster wheels for sturdy positioning.

The steps taken to design this were:

1. Need analysis for the product.
2. Creation of multiple concepts and selection of one.
3. Selection of material using the weighted point method.
4. Calculation for dimensions using anthropometry and stress analysis.
5. Finite Element Analysis of the designed components.
6. Final assembly of the product.
7. Creation of Manufacturing plan.

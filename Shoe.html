Combat Shoe with Deflector

This was a research project that I worked on during my 5th semester at TIET. It was based on introducing a new composition of composite materials to the soles of military combat shoes that would resist injury to bones under the impact of explosions.

This shoe sole is composed of 7 composite materials and also has a deflector in place to dissipate incoming explosive energy from the ground. In this project, more of the time was spent in the analysis than in designing. This project was analysed using the 'Explicit Dynamics' module of Ansys Workbench. This process took a little time, partly due to insufficient computing capacity, and partly due to my inexperience with Explicit Dynamics, but after this was done, I came out learning a lot about how it is done in the software.

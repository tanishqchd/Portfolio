R.C. Helicopter

This is one of the projects i found very fun and challenging to do. I took this up in the winter break after my 3rd semester and finished it before the beginning of my 4th semester. At this time, i had recently learned SolidWorks and this project help me design using curves. Also, the frontal part of this design, which is the windshield of the helicopter was made using surface modelling.

It took me a lot of hours to get the dimensions of different parts so as they fit together. That said, this model uses one estimated dimensions, but it helped me majorly in learning the complex process of 3D modelling, surface modelling and assembly in SolidWorks.
